Massimiliano Ciaramita


Short bio, Google Scholar profile, Google Research page

Software
  • Supersense tagger: (with Yasemin Altun) an implementation of Michael Collins perceptron HMM for broad sense disambiguation (Wordnet supersenses), PoS tagging and named entity recognition.
  • DeSR: (contributor) Giuseppe Attardi's transition-based multilingual dependency parser.
Data
  • Semantically annotated snapshot of Wikipedia: a snapshot of Wikipedia annotated with PoS tags, Wordnet supersenses, named entities and syntactic dependency relations.
Prototypes
  • Correlator: a prototype built at the Yahoo! Research Barcelona lab to explore new ways of information browsing based on NLP pre-processing and specialized ranking methods.
